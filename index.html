About me

I am an Associate Software Engineer at Moody's Analytics with over 7 years of experience specializing in Angular, ASP.NET, and SQL Server. My expertise extends to full-stack development using C#, .NET Core, and PostgreSQL. I am particularly passionate about leveraging GenAI tools; I recently developed a Back-Patch tool using GenAI to streamline code management.

Prior to Moody's, I honed my skills at TalentQuest, delivering robust internal applications and reporting systems using Angular, Kendo UI, and SSRS. I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. I am always eager to learn new technologies and have recently been recognized with the 'Extra Mile Award' for my contributions.
